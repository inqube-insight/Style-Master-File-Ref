1 Overview

Style Master File (SMF) is a R Shiny application, developed primarily to streamline the Bulk Order Processing flow between the Marketing, PDC, Costing, and COE teams.

SMF App was developed to reduce the duplication of work, reduce the amount of workload and provide a more easy to use interface for handling higher volumes of orders.
